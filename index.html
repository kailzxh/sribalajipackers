Frequently asked questions in Bangalore

Does Sri Balaji Packers and Movers handle local shifting or inter-city moves?

Yes, we do manage both Local as well as intercity relocation requirements

What sets Sri Balaji Packers and Movers apart from other logistics providers?

Sri Balaji Packers and Movers employs a cutting-edge technology platform tailored for household goods relocation services, ensuring unmatched user experiences and top-tier customer service. They assign a dedicated Move Manager to oversee the entire relocation process and deploy skilled, professional teams who utilize premium packing materials for secure handling of goods.

When is the best time to relocate ?

Do consider all the factors when deciding on the best time for you to move. If you have some flexibility in your schedule, provide us with a three day notice so that every aspect of the move is discussed in detail. If possible, be flexible with your destination time frame as well. Keep in mind that there could be some unforeseen problem en route due to weather and road conditions. For your reference - summer months (mid-March to mid-September),weekends and the beginning and end of each month (regardless of the season) are particularly busy and we recommend its better to do advance booking of slots.

When can I book my move ? How many days before ?

You need to book the order at least 1 day prior to the move.

Will the movers and packers also dismantle beds and other furniture ?

The movers are trained in basic carpentry, and they will dismantle and assemble the furniture which requires basic expertise using tools like allen keys and screwdrivers. Any modular or hydraulic furniture which were installed by trained carpenters with specialized tools, will require the assistance of an expert, and can be availed at request.
